# NumisHub - User Journeys v0.1

**Version:** 0.1
**Status:** Draft
**Audience:** Product, Design, Engineering

---

# Purpose

This document defines the primary user journeys for NumisHub.

Every feature, API endpoint and database model should support these journeys.

If a feature does not improve one of these journeys, it should be questioned.

---

# Product Mission

> **NumisHub is the easiest way for young professionals to understand, manage and grow their wealth—without spreadsheets.**

---

# Primary Persona

**Young Professional**

* Age: 22–35
* Has a salary
* Uses one or more bank accounts
* Saves money every month
* Invests in ETFs, stocks or crypto
* Wants a complete view of their finances
* Doesn't enjoy spreadsheets
* Wants automation, not manual work

---

# Core Product Principles

Every journey should reinforce these principles.

* Mobile-first
* AI-native
* Privacy-first
* Beautiful by default
* Fast enough to feel instant
* Automation over manual work
* Portuguese-first with global expansion in mind

# MVP Scope

## Included

* Authentication
* Dashboard
* Accounts
* Transactions
* Categories
* Investments
* Goals
* Manual entries
* CSV imports
* AI insights
* Net worth tracking

## Excluded

* Accounting
* Tax filing
* Bill payments
* Trading
* Social features
* Shared accounts
* Marketplace
* Loans and mortgages
* Advanced retirement planning

---

# North Star Experience

Within five minutes of installing NumisHub, the user should be able to understand:

* How much they are worth.
* Where their money is.
* Where their money goes.
* How their investments are performing.
* Whether they are making progress toward their goals.
* What they should pay attention to today.

If NumisHub consistently answers those six questions better than any spreadsheet or banking app, the product succeeds.
